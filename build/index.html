Contemporary Arts Center

One of the reasons we put QCM together was to showcase our favorite city, so of course we're hosting in an amazing venue downtown. The Contemporary Arts Center has awesome architecture, is full of great exhibits (did we mention that's included in your ticket?) and a crazy 45 staircase!

We've booked the venue for Thursday and Friday, so we're working on a great event for Thursday night with some familiar faces from around the city. That's included, too.
